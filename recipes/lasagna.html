Lasagna Recipe

Here is what my lasagna dinner looks like after it is done!

This meal is easy to make, and tastes great! However, it does take quite some time to prepare. Hope you ENJOY!

Ingredients:

  • Three pieces of Chicken Drumstick
  • Bowl of Romaine Lettuce
  • Bag of Croutons
  • Cesar Salad Dresssing
  • Garlic Powder
  • Basil Leaves
  • Chicken Seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash your chicken thoroughly.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F, and set the timer to 1h 20 minutes.
  3. Thoroughly pour the three spices one by one on all chicken, letting them soak for 10 minutes.
  4. After marinading is complete, put chicken in the oven and bake
  5. After that is done, turn the drumsticks over and add another 25 minutes to the oven.
  6. Wash your lettuce, and make your salad using the lettuce, croutons, and cesar salad sauce.
  7. Add your cooked chicken to your salad, and enjoy your dinner!
